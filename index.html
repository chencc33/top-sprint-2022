Let's protect yourself and others during extreme heat in Arizona.

How can I get help?

Temperatures in Arizona during the summer soar, and heat-related illeness and death are common.

Following below safety tips and using below resources when needed can help you and your family stay safe during the extreme heat.

How can I help?

You can help prevent heat-related illeness and deaths by checking on people, offering to drive them to an cooling location, donating cases of water, and volunteering at heat relief stations.
